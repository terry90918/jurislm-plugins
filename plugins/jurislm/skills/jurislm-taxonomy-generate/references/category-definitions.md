## Quality Guidelines

### Synonym Selection Criteria

1. **Semantic Equivalence**: Synonyms must be semantically equivalent or very closely related
2. **Taiwan Context**: All terms must be used in Taiwan legal practice
3. **Variety**: Include formal terms, colloquial terms, and common abbreviations
4. **Specificity**: Avoid overly broad or generic terms

### Term Sources

- Taiwan court judgments (司法院裁判書)
- Legal codes and regulations (法規資料庫)
- Legal academic literature
- Legal practice terminology

### Common Patterns

1. **Formal + Colloquial**: 竊盜 (formal) + 偷竊 (colloquial)
2. **Full + Abbreviated**: 著作權 (full) + 版權 (abbreviated)
3. **Legal + Layman**: 被告 (legal) + 當事人 (layman)
4. **Code + Practice**: 侵占罪 (code) + 挪用公款 (practice)
